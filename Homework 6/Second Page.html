Barry the Bear and the Coffee Shop Incident

A short story by ChatGPT

Barry was just your average 600-pound grizzly with a deep love for two things: naps and espresso. Not coffee, mind you espresso. Barry had developed the taste after raiding a campsite and slurping an unattended mocha. It changed his life.

One morning, Barry wandered into the small mountain town of Pinebucket and strolled right into **Clawfee House**, the local coffee shop. The barista, Kyle, dropped his oat milk.

Can I help you? Kyle asked, slowly reaching for the bear spray.

Barry grunted, then carefully pointed to the menu with one massive claw.

Double espresso, Kyle whispered. Got it.

To everyone's surprise, Barry sat politely at a corner table, paws folded, waiting.

When the espresso was served double shot, extra hot Barry took one sip, let out a pleased snort, and gave Kyle a pawful of pinecones as payment.

He left without mauling anyone.

The next day, Clawfee House had a new chalkboard sign:

Bear-friendly. Espresso served daily. No decaf. Ever.
